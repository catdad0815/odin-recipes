Raspberry Lemonade

Description

This raspberry lemonade is a riff on the fresh-squeezed lemonade I used to love to drink when visiting amusement parks during childhood summers.

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups fresh squeezed lemon juice from 8-12 lemons, strained
  • 1/2 pound (8oz.) raspberries, plus more for serving
  • 5 cups water
  • 1 cup sugar
  • Lemon wedges, for serving
  • Fresh mint, for serving

Instructions

  1. Combine the raspberries and 1/2 cup of lemon juice in a blender
  2. Puree until smooth
  3. Pass mixture through a sieve into a large bowl to strain out seeds (the fastest way to force it through is by pressing a ladle against the strainer in a circular motion).
