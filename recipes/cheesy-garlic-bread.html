Cheesy Garlic Bread

Description

The Best Cheesy Garlic Bread has 3 kinds of cheese, fresh herbs and tons of garlic. You can turn plain french bread into cheesy, garlicky perfection with this easy homemade garlic bread recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf french bread
  • 1/2 butter softened
  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsley divided
  • 5 cloves garlic minced
  • 1 cup fresh mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 fresh grated sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup fresh grated parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Slice french bread in half lengthwise. Place, each half, cut side up, on a foil lined baking sheet
  2. Stir together softened butter, minced garlic, and 1 tablespoon fresh chopped parsley. Spread evenly on each half of the french bread loaf. In a small bowl combine cheese then sprinkly evenly on top of the bread. Sprinkle remaining parsley evenly over the cheeses.
  3. Bake in 350 degree oven for 10 minutes. Turn on the broiler to high, move the rack position, and broiler for the last 2-3 minutes until cheese is golden and bubbly. (*The broiler step is optional-you could just let it bake for the 10 minutes).
